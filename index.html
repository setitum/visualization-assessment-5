Sports Shoe Brands Statistics

The Sports Shoes Brands dataset contains a simple list of popular sports shoe brands. Specifically, the dataset includes brand names, their webpage, social media links and statistics (Twitter, Instagram, etc.). It also includes other data such as market cap, revenue from different sources, and many more useful insights about each brand.

About the Chart

This chart shows the number of Twitter followers for top sports shoe brands. Nike leads by a significant margin, followed by Adidas and Puma.
